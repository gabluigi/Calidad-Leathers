Penny loafers are a timeless and versatile slip-on shoe that gained popularity in the mid-20th century. Their defining feature is a leather strap across the vamp with a small slit where, traditionally, wearers would place a penny for good luck—hence the name. Originating from Norwegian fisherman shoes, they became a staple of Ivy League style in America during the 1950s. Penny loafers are known for their casual elegance, making them suitable for both business-casual and leisure settings. With their easy slip-on design and clean silhouette, they strike the perfect balance between comfort and sophistication.
